Mac and Cheese

Dirty Mike's World Famous Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of pasta(elbows or shells
  • 1 lb. of Velveeta cheese
  • whole milk
  • 3 tbsp. of cream cheese

Steps

  1. bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. add pasta and cook until al dente 8-10 minutes, reduce heat as needed, stir occasionally
  3. while pasta cooks, cut Velveeta into 1/2"-1" cubes
  4. once pasta is cooked, strain out the water and return pasta to the same pot
  5. add Velveeta and cream cheese and stir until fully melted
  6. add milk as needed for desired consistency
  7. serve and enjoy
