Quick and Easy Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of spaghetti pasta
  • 12 oz of your favorite red sauce
  • 1 lb. of ground beef
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. add pasta and cook until al dente 8-10 minutes, reduce heat as needed, stir occasionally
  3. while pasta cooks, heat a splash of olive oil in a large pan on medium heat, and add the ground beef
  4. break beef up into small pieces and cook through
  5. once pasta is done, strain out water and return pasta to pot
  6. once beef is cooked, add sauce to pan with beef and stir continuously until it reaches a simmer
  7. add meat sauce to pot with pasta and mix thoroughly
  8. serve and enjoy
