PB&J Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. use a knife to spread peanut butter to one slice of bread
  2. use a knife to spread jelly to the other slice of bread
  3. put the slices of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly are touching
  4. serve and enjoy
